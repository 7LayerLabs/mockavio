## FAQ Section

### Headline
**Common Questions**

**Q: How accurate are the AI transformations?**
A: Mockavio's AI is trained on professional design and staging. Results are photorealistic and maintain proper spatial relationships, lighting, and scale. Many users report clients can't distinguish AI staging from real staging.

**Q: Do I own the images I create?**
A: Yes! All images created with Mockavio belong to you. Use them in listings, presentations, marketing materials, social media - anywhere you need them. Professional and Enterprise plans include commercial licensing.

**Q: Can I remove the Mockavio branding?**
A: Professional and Enterprise plans include white label exports. Add your own logo or export with no branding at all.

**Q: What if my client wants changes?**
A: Make unlimited revisions at no extra cost. Try different colors, styles, layouts - whatever your client needs to see. No per-image fees, ever.

**Q: How long does a transformation take?**
A: Most transformations complete in 30-60 seconds. Batch processing can handle 50+ images in a few minutes.

**Q: Can multiple team members use the same account?**
A: Professional plans include 5 users. Enterprise plans include unlimited users. Each team member gets their own login and workspace.

**Q: Is there a limit on file size?**
A: We support images up to 10MB. For best results, use high-quality photos at least 1920x1080 resolution.

**Q: What happens if I go over my monthly image limit?**
A: We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. You can upgrade anytime or purchase additional image packs. We'll never stop your account mid-project.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime with one click. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: Do you offer training?**
A: All plans include email support and access to our video tutorial library. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support. Enterprise plans include white-glove onboarding and training sessions.
